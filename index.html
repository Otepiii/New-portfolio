Hi I'm Joseph Evangelista


As a highly motivated and skilled technology professional, I have a diverse background in web development and support engineering. I began my career as a Junior Developer at Projectpartner, where I was responsible for building a marketing site and fixing bugs. It was there that I laid a strong foundation in web development.

Following that, I took on a customer-facing role as a Support Engineer at Mindtree. There, I provided support to Microsoft Azure clients, solved technical issues, and collaborated with cross-functional teams. I enjoyed the challenge of finding solutions to complex problems and working closely with customers to provide the best support possible.

Currently, I work as an Azure Support Engineer at Allyis Inc, where I am committed to supporting critical workloads, managing the customer experience, and collaborating with subject matter experts. I take pride in my ability to work under pressure and prioritize tasks to meet tight deadlines.

I am also passionate about learning and continuing my education. My coding boot camp certificate from the University of Miami and my Bachelor's degree in International Hospitality Management are a testament to my commitment to personal and professional growth. In my free time, I always try to learn and expand my skillsets by working on getting certificates whether it is Azure, Cloud or Linux.

Overall, I believe I am a driven and well-rounded professional with a strong foundation in technology and a commitment to personal and professional growth.
